How to Update

1
Connect your phone or laptop to your FieldTunnel device WiFi
FieldTunnel-XXXX · password: fieldtunnel123
2
Open the web UI at http://192.168.4.1 or http://fieldtunnel-xxxx.local
3
Go to Firmware in the sidebar
4
Click Choose File and select the downloaded .bin file
5
Click Upload Firmware — progress bar shows upload status, device reboots automatically (~30 seconds)
6
Reconnect to the device and confirm the new version on the Dashboard

One-click updates: Devices with internet access can update automatically from the web UI.

Go to Firmware → Check for Updates → Update Now
