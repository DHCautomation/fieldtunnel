Use Cases

Where FieldTunnel works.

BAS Retrofit

Add IP connectivity to existing RS485 field buses without rewiring. Drop a FieldTunnel on the bus and bridge to your BAS head-end over WiFi.

SlateBAS Integration

FieldTunnel is a native SlateBAS edge device. Auto-discovered, zero-config protocol bridging from field devices to the SlateBAS platform.

VFD & Equipment

Connect variable frequency drives, boilers, chillers, and other Modbus equipment to IP networks without running new cable.

Field Testing

Portable protocol analysis. Connect to any RS485 bus from your laptop or phone over WiFi. Read registers, scan devices, debug communications.
